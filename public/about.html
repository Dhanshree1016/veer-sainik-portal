About Veer Sainik Portal

A tribute to our heroes through technology and service.

Our Vision

To become a leading force in revolutionizing military operations by empowering armed forces with innovative, tech-driven solutions that enhance operational effectiveness, situational awareness, and the safety of every soldier.

Our Mission

To design and deliver reliable, cutting-edge solutions for monitoring, situational awareness, and data-driven decision-making. We aim to ensure the safety, security, and success of soldiers in every mission, fostering an ecosystem of trust and technological excellence.

History

The portal was founded as a tribute to the valor of our armed forces. It aims to bridge the gap between citizens and their heroes by creating a digital repository of their incredible tales of service.

Key Features

  • Real-Time Location Tracking: GPS-based monitoring ensures precise positioning of soldiers in any terrain.
  • Health Monitoring: Tracks vital signs such as heart rate, body temperature, and external pressure.
  • Object Detection: Advanced sensor technology identifies nearby threats like drones or enemy movement.
  • Environmental Analysis: Measures pressure, altitude, and motion to support dynamic operations.
